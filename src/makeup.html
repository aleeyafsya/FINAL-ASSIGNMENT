Makeup Tips







Foundation before concealer

It will help minimize any redness or discoloration




Exfoliate your lips

Creating a smoother, softer canvas for your products




Blush all the way

It gives the skin that fresh and rejuvenated look and it adds the perfect amount of depth to the face




Heat your eyelash curler

The added heat will help your lashes curl faster and keep the shape for longer
